1. What is the primary goal of data visualization in the data analysis process?
To make calculations to explore the data and create visual representations of the data that allow to extract insights.

2. Define the stakeholder experience in the context of data analysis and visualization
The objective is to understand the group of people that will use the data. By doing so, our mission is to communicate information based on the user needs, requirements and expectations.

3. Explain two reasons why stakeholder experience is important when creating data visualizations.
- To make the data analysis process relevant to the stakeholder and easy to understand.
- To make impact in the company by providing high quality visualizations.

5. making questions to stakeholders, understanding the context of the company.

1. Ricci is the customer service manager. She is responsible for ensuring that customers are satisfied with the company's products and services.
- Influence: high
- Interest: high
- Reason: Ricci is aware of the customer satisfaction metrics. 

- Questions: 
	Cual es el top 3 de razones por las cuales los clientes nos contactan en os PQRs?
	Cual es el indice de satisfaccion por regiones?
	Como se califica el nivel de satisfaccion o insatisfaccion de los clientes?

2. Igor is the finance manager. He oversees the company's financial operations.
- Influence: low
- Interest; High
- Reason: Igor can forecast the company revenue based on the sales trends and historic data, but he has little knowledge in terms of customer KPI's.

- Questions:
	Como ha sido el comportamiento de las finanzas de la empresa desde que empezo a notarse la disminucion en ventas?

	Cual es la meta en crecimiento y ventas para el proximo ano? Que tan factible es esta meta?
